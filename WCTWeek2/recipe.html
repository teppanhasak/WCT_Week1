Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy Italian classic ready in 25 minutes

Ingredients

  1. 400g spaghetti
  2. 200g pancetta, diced
  3. 4 eggs (2 whole + 2 yolks)
  4. 100g Pecorino Romano, grated
  5. Black pepper and salt

Steps

  1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. Whisk eggs with cheese and black pepper.
  4. Toss hot pasta with pancetta, then pour egg mixture over it.
  5. Add a splash of pasta water to make it creamy. Serve immediately.
