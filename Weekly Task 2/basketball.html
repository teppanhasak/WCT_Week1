Basketball

About Basketball

Basketball is a team sport played by two teams of five players. The goal is to shoot the ball through the opponent's hoop to score points.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 5 players
  • Game has 4 quarters
  • Score by shooting the ball into the hoop
  • 3 points for shots beyond the arc, 2 inside
