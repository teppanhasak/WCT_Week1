Football

About Football

Football is one of the most popular sports in the world. It is played between two teams of 11 players each on a rectangular field.

Basic Rules

  • Two teams of 11 players
  • Game lasts 90 minutes
  • Score by getting the ball into the opponent's goal
  • No hands allowed (except the goalkeeper)
