Tennis

About Tennis

Tennis is a racket sport played between two players (singles) or two teams of two players (doubles). Players use a racket to hit a ball over a net into the opponent's court.

Basic Rules

  • Played as singles or doubles
  • Score: 15, 30, 40, game
  • First to win 6 games wins a set
  • Ball must land inside the court lines
